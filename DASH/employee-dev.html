Training & Seminar

Skills development and progressive learning is vital in DASH in order to attain its goal to excellence. Indulging in trainings, workshops and seminars are beneficial to the growth of the company and a great help to individual development as well.

First Aid Training

First aiders of the Emergency Response Team of DASH Engineering Philippines, Inc. (DASH) attended the first aid Training every 2nd Quarter of the year.

Certified trainers from the Emergency Rescue Unit Foundation (ERUF) facilitated the two-day activity.

Japanese Language Class

Dash Engineering Philippines Inc. (DASH)offers Japanese Language Learning Program to employees who are enthusiastic to learn the foreign language. With a competent language instructor the program accommodates an average of 30 employees within a year. Classes are held four hours a week, carefully arranged so as not to be in any conflict with the work schedule.

The program intends to provide proficiency of the language, better relationships with the Japanese counterparts and clients, a deeper understanding of the Japanese culture and a sense of personal fulfillment, all to the individual's maximum benefit.

Calligraphy

Elegant black ink ripples and flows smoothly as the brush is lifted, then its tip gracefully glides on the mulberry paper or washi, following curvilinear strokes to form “kanji” characters symbolizing an iceberg, a citrus fruit, a pigeon, well-being and good life. The ancient art of Japanese Calligraphy displayed DASH team members’ artistic soul as a part of the Japanese New Year celebration held every year in Dash office.

Happy 25th Anniversary

Years have shaped DASH to be resilient when global competition had been tough; enduring when others were shaken; diverse when others remained the same; visionary for what others thought impossible.

As we step into this major milestone, we thank those who have joined us in the past, those who have stayed with us until today, and to those who have just been part of our family recently.

In celebration of our 25th Anniversary, series of activities were lined up to commemorate this significant event. The first working day of April, marked the start of the month long celebration with special treats given to all employees.

April 08 highlighted the 3D "Kong: Skull Island" movie experience of all employees at the Large Screen format, SM City Seaside. As we are thankful to all our employees, we have likewise extended our utmost gratitude to all families who took part of our Family Visit program, their presence made a difference in our ordinary Friday work life experience.

There is no better way of concluding the celebration of this memorable occasion with all our distinguished guests at the Marco Polo Plaza hotel last April 28, 2017.

This is one for the books and we look forward to unfolding each milestone hereon with you.

A SPOOKY TREAT

Children's day is recognized on various days in many places around the world. The Universal Children's Day takes place annually every 20th of November. It celebrates children for being who they are.

In DASH, the Children's Day celebration was conducted last October 29, 2016. The employees' kids experienced being in the office. The children got to play, laugh and run together with other children. With an estimated 80 kids who came, they filled the office with much cuteness and joy.

The celebration didn't just end there, all kids were brought to a 'different dimension' with the help of SM Storyland and their fairy tale dream came to life.

Safety & Health

The safety and health of the employees are part of the priorities of DASH.

The company provided all necessary measures to guarantee its people of a safe workplace that they deserve.

Fire Drill

In its objectives to provide an orderly emergency response plan for all the occupants; to ensure fast, organized and smooth evacuation of the building during emergencies; and to train fire drill and emergency evacuation officers to conduct their duties successfully.

Fun Run

A company-wide fun run with unique themes is conducted every 2nd quarter of the year, handled by our Occupational Safety and Health Committee (OSHC). This friendly run aims to promote health awareness, strengthen comradeship and a stress-free lifestyle.
